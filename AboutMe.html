Sarah Owusu

☆Graphic Designer★

I’m Sarah Owusu, a London-based graphic designer and Camberwell College of Arts graduate. My work mixes fashion, music, and storytelling into expressive visuals that connect emotionally and challenge creative limits. I started out painting, moved into photography, and now use coding to bring design to life in new, interactive ways. My website is fully coded by me, a little proof that I love going beyond the expected to make ideas real. When I’m not working on projects, you’ll find me freestyling experiments on Instagram. A space where I play, test, and let creativity flow without rules.

☆ Always up for exciting collaborations and bold ideas—let’s create something unforgettable. ★
