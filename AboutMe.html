☆Graphic Designer★

I’m Sarah Owusu, a London-based graphic designer and Camberwell College of Arts graduate. My work is driven by storytelling. I believe every design should have meaning, from the initial idea and creative exploration to the outcome. My portfolio reflects this journey, showing the process behind each project as much as the result. I enjoy challenging myself creatively and experimenting with new techniques, because graphic design is a field where learning never stops. My path into design began with a love for painting and photography. It later expanded into coding, allowing me to bring my ideas, like my portfolio, to life through interactive and expressive visuals.

☆ Always up for exciting collaborations and bold ideas —> let’s create something unforgettable. ★
