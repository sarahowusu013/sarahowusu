Sarah Owusu

Graphic Designer

Hello, I’m Sarah Owusu, a London-based Graphic Designer and Camberwell College of Arts graduate. My work explores the power of expressive visuals to evoke meaningful connections, influenced by my diverse upbringing, passion for fashion, and love of music. Starting as a painter, then moving into photography, I’ve embraced multiple disciplines to push the boundaries of design. Recently, I’ve been exploring coding to create interactive and unconventional visuals that invite viewers into the design process. At the heart of my practice is storytelling, where I see design as a universal language that resonates with all.
