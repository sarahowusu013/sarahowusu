All the teams were tasked with designing a team kit for Team Gad, Team Ephraim, and Team Levi. This top was created as our Sunday live uniform to represent each team. Although my team, Team Gad, came first, the design still needed development as it didn’t fully resonate with everyone. I refined it by keeping the top black for a clean, cohesive look and experimented with placement, inspired by football shirts, with the badge on the right and the team name centred. The badge is embroidered for a high-quality finish, while the team name is printed for a bold, clear visual.
