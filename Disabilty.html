These posters were created along with an interactive booklet to inform people about the various different disabilities and their effect on the individual with the condition and what people can do to stay mindful of the different needs of the disabilities. This was a collaboration with UAL students who studied a different course but we all managed to work together well. We chose this topic because people with disabilities are often viewed as one group that includes all forms of disabilities. However, the community of people with disabilities is a diverse group with various needs. There are many disabilities that are visually apparent whilst others are not as evident known as invisible disabilities. We named the booklet ‘Stay Mindful’ which can be downloaded below. Through these posters, I wanted to show that people with disabilities are often mistreated because they are different from what society wants them to be. However, they are still able to achieve things like everyone else.
