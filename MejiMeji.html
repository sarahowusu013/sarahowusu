I collaborated with Meji Meji, a Nigerian clothing brand that celebrates culture through a sustainable and women-empowered lens. The brand sought to introduce storytelling to promote their clothing pieces, and I contributed by creating the illustrations for the story Reunited by the Crown—a narrative that reflects Nigerian culture. In addition, I designed the CDs for the audio version of the story and an accompanying playlist. I also supported the brand by helping design some of their upcoming clothing pieces and creating content for their social media, aligning everything with their commitment to sustainability and women’s empowerment.
